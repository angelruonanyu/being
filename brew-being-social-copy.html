Event title
Brew & Being at Moonstruck
Tagline
Come for the coffee. Stay for the people. Leave with something that matters.
Description
Brew & Being is a gathering of 12–15 people around coffee, tea, and honest conversation. On 9 May, we're at Moonstruck Cafe in London — one of the city's rare spaces where slowness is already a choice. We will lead a light-roast, single-origin coffee tasting and hand-brew Chinese tea. Then we'll eat rose Basque cheesecake together and talk, about what we're carrying, what we keep forgetting to notice, and how we want to live. Just a room of people who want to take life a little more seriously, together. The evening: 7:00 — Doors open 7:10 — Coffee tasting 7:30 — A moment of quiet 7:35 — Chinese tea + Living Well Initiative 8:20 — Rose Basque cheesecake + open conversation 8:50 — Close + linger Tickets include: coffee tasting, hand-brewed Chinese tea, rose Basque cheesecake. Capacity: 15 guests. Small by design. Hosted by: Angel Ruonan Yu (Being / Living Well Initiative), in collaboration with Moonstruck Cafe.
